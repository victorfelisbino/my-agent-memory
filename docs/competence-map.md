## How to read this page

- **Depth** is author-set in `competence-map.yml`. The generator refuses to render `expert` entries that have zero evidence or have not been touched in 180+ days.
- **Dormant** is computed: any entry not touched in 90+ days renders as dormant regardless of author-set depth, so a stale brain looks stale.
- **Evidence** counts are mechanical: file count, `## ` heading count, `gotcha:` / `lesson:` markers, total bytes. They measure substance, not quality.
- **Scope** = `shared` on this page. `personal-only` entries live in the sibling `my-agent-memory-personal` repo and never publish here.
- **Two-repo merge** (sibling personal repo) and **automatic regeneration** are roadmap follow-ups (Wave 2.5 follow-up section).
